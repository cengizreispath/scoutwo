# REVIEW_CHECKLIST.md
## Critical Review and Self-Challenge Checklist

### Problem / Scope Review
- [ ] Did I solve the real problem?
- [ ] Is scope clear?
- [ ] Did I accidentally add unrelated work?

### Design Review
- [ ] Is the approach consistent with project constraints?
- [ ] Did I consider a plausible alternative?
- [ ] Is there overengineering?

### Verification Review
- [ ] What if my main assumption is wrong?
- [ ] What could regress?
- [ ] What remains unproven?

### Operational Review
- [ ] What could fail in deployment?
- [ ] Are env/config needs clear?
- [ ] Is rollback plausible?

## Required Critique Output
- What I am confident about
- What may be wrong
- What still needs validation
- What I would check next in a real environment
